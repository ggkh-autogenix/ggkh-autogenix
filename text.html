The Challenge & Our Vision

This section outlines the core problem our Agentic AI system addresses and presents our innovative solution. We aim to transform traditional surveillance into a proactive tool for operational efficiency and intelligent resource management across various sectors.

Problem Statement

Facility managers lack intelligent, automated systems to monitor and optimize human resource usage in real-time from CCTV data. This leads to inefficient manpower deployment, unnoticed operational issues (like waste overflow or leaks), and unnecessary costs. Similar challenges exist in agriculture with crop health and resource management.

Invention Summary

Our Agentic AI Agent-powered Human Behavior and Utilization Monitoring System transforms CCTV/Drone infrastructure into an intelligent platform. It integrates advanced Object Detection (YOLOv8), Human Pose Estimation (MediaPipe/OpenPose), custom behavior recognition models, and a real-time alert/task assignment engine to provide deep operational insights.

Proposed Solution: How It Works

The Agentic AI platform analyzes human activities, detects idle personnel, recommends tasks, and issues real-time anomaly alerts, all by processing existing camera feeds.
